Select one of the four AI Traffic Lights to see the specific rules and expectations for using Artificial Intelligence in your university assignments.

  • ● Red — Prohibited
  • ● Yellow — Allowed, but must be reported
  • ● Green — Allowed, no report needed
  • ● Blue — Required, must be used and reported

This model helps you manage academic integrity and leverage AI effectively.
